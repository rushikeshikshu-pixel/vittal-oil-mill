User & Operations Manual

Complete step-by-step instructions for running the Vitthal Oil Mill ERP.

Introduction & Quick Start

This ERP system manages the daily weighing, production logs, maintenance schedules, and party ledgers for Vitthal Oil Mill. It is designed to run offline on your local network while maintaining real-time backup synchronisation with the Google Firebase Cloud database.

🚀 Quick Navigation Tip: Click any section on the left sidebar to jump directly to its instructions, or scroll through this page to read the complete guide.

Cotton Seed Unloads

Use this section to record raw cotton seed deliveries arriving at the mill's weighbridge.

  • Click "Record Lorry Entry" at the top right of the Unloads tab.
  • Enter the Invoice Weight (sent by the supplier) and the actual Net Received Weight weighed at your scale.
  • The system automatically computes any weight Shortage and displays a warning if it exceeds limits.
  • Specify the quantity and type of packing bags (plastic or jute). The system automatically tracks your bag inventory.

Finished Product Dispatches

Log deliveries of oil tanks or cake/hulls trucks dispatched to customers.

  • Click "Record Dispatch Entry".
  • Select the target customer and product type (e.g. Wash Oil, Crude Oil, Cake/Khal, Hulls).
  • Enter weight, sale rate, and transport lorry number.
  • Saving a dispatch automatically deducts the volume from your physical product stocks.

Expeller Crushing Logs

Log daily crushing runs to measure mechanical yield and oil recovery rates.

  • Click "Log Crushing Run".
  • Select the active Seed Lorry lot being crushed and enter total weight fed into the expeller.
  • Input finished output weights: Crude Oil, Cake/Khal, and Cotton Hulls.
  • The system calculates the recovery yield percentage automatically. Standard runs should achieve 98%+ yield recovery.

Oil Refining Logs

Tracks chemical processing runs where raw Crude Oil is processed into Refined Wash Oil.

  • Click "Log Refining Batch".
  • Input the weight of Crude Oil consumed, and the finished outputs (Wash Oil, Soap Stock, and Acid Oil).
  • The system calculates the refining process weight loss and loss percentage dynamically.

Party Ledger Accounts

Provides full financial details for each supplier (whom you buy seeds from) and customer (whom you sell oil/cake to).

  • Select a supplier or customer name to load their live transactions list.
  • Unloads add to supplier balances, while sales add to customer balances.
  • To log payments (bank RTGS, cash, or cheques), click "Record Payment/Payout" at the top right of the Party Accounts tab.

Stock Statement Ledger

Displays active physical inventories of raw seed, oils, cake, and hulls.

  • Stocks are computed automatically in real-time from your logged purchases, crushing yields, and sales.
  • If you perform a physical audit and find discrepancy, click on any stock cell directly to enter a Manual Override.
  • Click "Reset to Auto" on the overrides grid to restore database-driven automatic calculations.

Spare Parts & Maintenance Logs

Track machinery upkeep, breakdown repairs, and parts stock levels.

  • The **Spare Parts** catalog lists all expeller blades, cones, and gaskets. Low stock items show red alerts.
  • When logging a repair job, specify which spare parts were consumed. Saving the repair automatically deducts those parts from the inventory catalog.
  • Click **"Register Spare Part"** or **"Record Repair Job"** to add entries.

Transport Fleet Tracking

Tracks diesel fuel expenses and operation hours for JCB excavators and delivery trucks.

  • Tracks fuel efficiency metrics: **km/L** for transport trucks and **Litres/Hour** for heavy excavators.
  • Click **"Log Vehicle Entry"** to record refuels or routine maintenance details.

Bill Builder & Invoice Generator

Generate GST tax invoices for completed dispatches.

  • Select a Customer and click **"Create Invoice"**.
  • Select the dispatches you want to bill from the checkboxes list.
  • Choose GST rate ($5\%$ or $18\%$), add custom discount or freight additions, and click **"Generate Invoice"**.
  • You can instantly print or export the generated invoice as a clean PDF document.

Role Access & Security

Enforce access controls for your staff to secure the dashboard views.

  • Turn on security by ticking **"Require role login on this device"** under the **Database** tab.
  • Staff will be prompted with a secure login keypad to enter their PIN.
  • The system hides sensitive tabs (like Invoices, Ledgers, Database, and Analytics) for lower roles (Weighbridge and Supervisor). Only Anand (Owner) can view all tabs and delete entries.

Database & System Backups

Export logs to local files, upload previous backups, seed standard data, or wipe system state.

Backup Database

Export all unloads, sales, stock ledgers, spare parts listings, and repair histories into a single JSON file. Ideal for offline storage.

Restore Database

Load database records back into the browser. Selecting a JSON backup file will overwrite the current local state.

Reset System

Seed the system with sample values to test out all functionalities, or clear out the current database cache completely.

Roles & Access (RBAC)

Assign staff a role and a PIN. Each role sees only its own tabs; only the Admin can delete records. This is a workflow control, not encryption — anyone with the database file can still read it.
